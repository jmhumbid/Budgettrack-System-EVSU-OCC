Clear Utilization LocalStorage

Instructions:
  1. Enter the Department ID (e.g., 15 for Education)
  2. Click "Clear LocalStorage" to remove all utilization data for that department
  3. Or click "Clear All LocalStorage" to remove all utilization data
⚠️ Warning: This will only clear data from your browser's localStorage. To remove the entry from the database, use the deletion feature in the utilization page, or contact your administrator.
To clear from Database:

Open the browser console (F12) and run:

fetch('../api/manual_delete_entry.php', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {'Content-Type': 'application/json'},
    body: JSON.stringify({
        category_name: 'ENTRY 1',
        department_id: 15,
        fiscal_year: 2026
    })
}).then(r => r.json()).then(console.log);
